=== Starfish ===

The Starfish trinket is available from [[Atlantis]], the [[Cursed Isles]], and the [[Haunted Seas]] in all of the [[Trinket_palette#Cloth-based|cloth colors]] except inky and night blue.  It has also been available from promotions in all the [[Color#Awarded_in_promotions|promotional colors]].  The indigo starfish was only available from tournaments around the time that the Cursed Isles were initially released.
